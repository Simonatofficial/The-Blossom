# 06 — The COSMOS Method

*A cosmic-floral framework for cultivating habits and goals with intention and steady growth. Built on the foundations of Atomic Habits (identity, habit stacking, make-it-easy/attractive/satisfying, never-miss-twice), implementation intentions, and tiny-habits research — wrapped in The Blossom's gentle aesthetic.*

COSMOS powers the **Habit widget wizard** and the **Goal widget**, and stands alone as a worksheet the user can revisit.

## Principles (why it works)

1. **Clarity brings gravity.** A vague wish has no pull; define exactly what orbits your focus, and why.
2. **Growth blooms slowly.** Start smaller than feels worthwhile — the tiny version sustains the momentum that the ambitious version kills.
3. **Harmony of rhythm.** New habits take root fastest when grafted onto patterns you already live.
4. **Make it radiant.** Lower friction, add immediate reward and a little beauty — the brain repeats what feels good *now*.
5. **Observe and realign.** Reflection is not a report card; it's pruning. You're tending a garden, not passing a test.

## The six steps

| | Step | Question it answers |
|---|---|---|
| **C** | **Clarify** | What exactly am I growing, and why does it matter? |
| **O** | **Orient** | When, where, and in what state will I do it? |
| **S** | **Stack** | What existing moment will it attach to? |
| **M** | **Motivate** | What friction can I remove; what reward can I add? |
| **O** | **Observe** | What does tiny / normal / inspired look like, and what milestones mark the path? |
| **S** | **Study** | When and how will I reflect and adjust? |

**Trigger formula:** *After I **[ANCHOR]**, at **[PLACE]** around **[TIME]**, I will **[TINY ACTION → STANDARD ACTION]**. Then I'll **[REWARD]**.*

## The in-app wizard (Habit widget creation)

One step per screen, soft progress dots, every field skippable except name + trigger. Each screen has a one-line example beneath the input in `textSoft`.

1. **Clarify** — Purpose ("why it matters", one sentence) · Goal ("what you want") · Metric ("how you'll know", links naturally to a Tracker output).
2. **Orient** — Day chips (M T W Th F Sa Su) · time window · place/environment · desired feeling (a short word-bank: steady, grounded, luminous, playful… or freeform).
3. **Stack** — Pick or type an anchor habit; the wizard composes the trigger sentence live as a preview card. Includes a "Recipes" link with starter stacks (after watering plants → 1 MVV of [skill]; after first phone unlock → 1 breath exercise; after keys hit the bowl → 2-min gear prep; after closing the laptop → lay out tomorrow's clothes).
4. **Motivate** — Remove **two frictions** (two fields, with prep-the-night-before suggestions) · add **two rewards** (instant + meaningful; can reference a Market custom reward) · **If/Then backup**: "If I miss my window, I'll ______ within 24h."
5. **Observe** — Define three tiers: **MVV** (minimum viable version, 2–5 min — the streak-saver), **Standard** (a normal day), **Stretch** (when inspired). Add milestones with dates (these surface in Calendar + Goal widgets).
6. **Study** — Choose weekly target (e.g. 5/7) · review day/time (creates a gentle recurring reminder) · done. The wizard plants the habit with a small bloom animation.

**Quick-Start Card:** after creation, the widget can render a one-line summary anywhere (copyable): *After I [ANCHOR] at [TIME/PLACE], I'll [MVV → STANDARD]. If I miss, [BACKUP within 24h]. Logged in [TRACKER], reviewed [DAY/TIME].*

**3-2-1 Bloom Start** (offered right after the wizard): **3** minutes — do the MVV right now · **2** frictions — clear them before you begin · **1** reward — take it when done. Starting today, however small, beats starting perfectly.

## Daily use

- The card's tier buttons (MVV / Standard / Stretch) make logging one tap. *Any* tier keeps the streak — MVV exists precisely so bad days still count.
- Missed day → the backup plan surfaces gently the next morning ("Yesterday slipped. Your backup: ____ — still within reach?"). Never-miss-twice copy, never guilt copy.

## Weekly review (Study)

At the chosen review time, the Habit widget surfaces a review card:

- The week at a glance (7 dots: tier-colored, wilted for misses) and target hit/missed.
- Three prompts: *What grew strongest this week? What wilted or needs pruning? What will I carry forward?* (saved as a dated reflection object; journalable).
- **Grade & adapt** (kept honest, applied softly):
  - **A** — ≥90% of planned sessions, MVV never skipped → offer to raise Standard.
  - **B** — 75–89% → steady; carry on.
  - **C** — 60–74% → suggest shrinking MVV and removing one more friction.
  - **R (re-root)** — <60% → suggest changing anchor, time, or place. Re-rooting is presented as gardening wisdom, not failure.

## Recording & Reflecting (the two halves of the system)

**Recording** — the structure: planner views (Calendar), routines, daily schedule, trackers — capturing goals, routines, and reflections daily across all four aspects (Physical, Mental, Emotional, Social) and keeping every active habit/goal visible in one rhythm.

**Reflecting** — the growth: journaling, free writing, sketching, brainstorming; reviewing what worked and what didn't; turning insight into adjusted habits and new goals. The Journal widget's prompts, the weekly review, and Goal reflection notes all feed this. The Blossom system itself should be refined as understanding deepens — reflection applies to the garden *and* the gardener.

## Aesthetic notes

- Symbol: a cosmos flower inside an orbit ring — petals as the six steps, ring as the growth cycle. Use as the Habit widget's icon and wizard header sigil (inline SVG).
- Wizard palette leans deep green + pink-gold over a dotted star-map texture, regardless of theme (subtly blended with theme colors).
- Tagline available for empty states: *"Help your future blossom."*

## Worked example (bundled as a sample Blossom code)

Rock climbing finger strength — **C:** grip strength for harder routes & injury prevention; +10 lb max hang on 20mm edge by Dec 15; metric = added weight for 7s × 5 sets weekly. **O:** M/W/F, 7:00–7:20 AM, doorway hangboard, feeling steady/grounded/luminous. **S:** after putting the breakfast mug in the sink → 2-min warm-up + one MVV hang. **M:** frictions removed = chalk & timer laid out, DND + 10-min timer; rewards = streak tick + 5 min favorite music; backup = 2 MVV sets at 5:30 PM. **O:** MVV = warm-up + 1×7s easy hang; Standard = 5×7s @ working weight; Stretch = +1–2 sets or +2.5 lb if RPE ≤7; milestones +5 lb Nov 15, +10 lb Dec 15. **S:** 3 sessions/week target, review Sunday 6 PM.
